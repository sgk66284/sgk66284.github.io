Ducati

Ducati 1199 Panigale S by Shade Fotoworks on flickr.com

Ducati is the quintessential Italian motorbike company. Across their extensive cataloge there are machines from all segments of the industry with a unique flair that can only be described as "capolavoro". Above anything else in their line stands the Panigales in variable engine displacements. These are street legal race bikes that are sure to envoke many smiles per twist of the throttle. Powere is even felt in the brand's distinct pain schemes. For their most famous models, they come wearing a coat of 'Rosso Corsa'. This happens to be the same flavor of red that car companies like Alpha Romeo and Ferrari use on their supercars.
