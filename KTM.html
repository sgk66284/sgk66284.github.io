KTM

KTM by Nicolas O. Pascual on flickr.com

KTM is a completely different animal from all of the other manufactures featured here because KTM cuts their teeth most heavily in the off road segment. More than half of their product range is dedicated to dual sports and enduroes meant to be taken on serious adventures across the rockiest terrain. It seems almost by design,then, that these bikes are most often a bright orange color; calling attention to themselves more quickly than a gray BMW of red Ducati. In the event of a spill on the trail, this coloring techinque will be sure to alert others to one's presence.
